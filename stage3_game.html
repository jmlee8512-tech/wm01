읽기 지문 (패러프레이징 결과)
출처: https://www.unep.org/news-and-stories/opinion/threads-change
Every piece of clothing is made of thousands of threads. Each thread represents a choice — from how the cloth is made to how we buy and wear it. The fashion industry has a huge effect on our environment. This year, International Zero Waste Day is focusing on the fashion and textile industry. It highlights the urgent need for more sustainable ways to make and use clothing. Every year, the fashion industry produces enormous amounts of waste and pollution. Fast fashion encourages people to buy more clothes than they need and throw them away quickly. This creates serious problems for the environment. To solve these problems, we need to change the way we think about clothing. We can choose to buy fewer items, select higher-quality pieces, or purchase secondhand clothes. Recycling and reusing textiles are also important steps toward a more sustainable future. By making smarter choices, each of us can help reduce the impact of the fashion industry on our planet.
