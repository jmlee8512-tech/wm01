읽기 문제 (GPT · Gemini 생성)
문제: 다음 빈칸에 들어갈 말로 가장 알맞은 것은?
Today, the fashion industry has a big impact on our environment. Every year, it creates serious pollution and waste. That is why International Zero Waste Day has chosen fashion and textiles as its focus. We need to find better, more _______ ways to make and use clothes.
① sudden
② empty
③ sustainable
④ expensive
⑤ common
정답: ③
해설: 앞에서 패션 산업이 환경에 큰 영향을 주고 심각한 오염과 쓰레기를 만든다고 했으므로, 더 나은 '지속 가능한' 방법을 찾아야 한다는 흐름이 자연스럽다.
출제 범위: 1문단 마지막 2문장
듣기 활동 문제 (GPT · Gemini 생성)
What was the boy going to do with his hoodie?
a. Give it to his uncle
b. Throw it away
c. Buy a new one
Explanation: The boy says that he was going to throw the hoodie away because it had a hole in the sleeve.
Answer: b
